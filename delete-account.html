Delete your Limitless account & data

Effective date: May 29, 2026

You can request deletion of your Limitless account and associated personal data at any time. We will process verified requests within 30 days.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address linked to your Limitless account to:

teretz@gmail.com

Include:

  • Subject line: Limitless account deletion request
  • The Google account email you use to sign in to Limitless
  • Optional: reason for deletion (helps us improve the app)

In the app

  1. Open Limitless → Settings (account icon).
  2. Tap Delete account to sign out on your device.
  3. Email us at the address above to permanently delete your cloud account and synced data.

What we delete

  • Your authentication account and profile (Supabase)
  • Synced settings, including birth date stored in our backend
  • Associated app user identifiers used for sync

What may remain

  • Local data on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app storage.
  • Subscription records retained by Google Play and RevenueCat as required for billing, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
  • Ad-related data held by Google AdMob according to Google’s policies (manage via your device ad settings).
  • Chat messages sent to Groq (if you used Astrology Chat) may be retained by Groq under their policies.

Questions

See our Privacy Policy or contact teretz@gmail.com.

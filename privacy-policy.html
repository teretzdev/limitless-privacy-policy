Privacy Policy for Limitless

Effective date: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Limitless ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Limitless mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Birth date you enter to generate astrology and biorhythm calculations.
  • Account data (such as email and auth identifiers) when you sign in.
  • Optional chat content you type in the Astrology Chat feature.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic app/device diagnostics and service metadata from third-party providers used by the app.
  • Ad-related information when ads are shown (for free-tier users).
  • Purchase/subscription status for premium access.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features (charts, transits, influence timeline, and personalization).
  • To save your preferences and restore your app state.
  • To support authentication, account features, and cloud sync (where enabled).
  • To provide subscription entitlements and remove ads for premium users.
  • To provide optional AI-assisted chat responses.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Storage and Processing

  • Some data is stored locally on your device (for example app preferences and locally cached values).
  • If you use account features, data may be stored in backend services associated with the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Limitless may use third-party providers, including:

  • Supabase for authentication and app data storage.
  • Google AdMob for ads in the free tier.
  • RevenueCat for subscription management.
  • Groq for optional Astrology Chat responses.

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers as needed to operate the app, process subscriptions, serve ads, provide optional AI chat, and comply with law.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can remove app-local data by uninstalling the app and clearing app storage.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may choose not to sign in and still use local-only features (when available).
  • You can change or remove your birth date in-app.
  • You can request account/data deletion at our account deletion page or from in-app Settings.
  • You can control ad personalization settings through your device/Google settings where applicable.

8. Children’s Privacy

Limitless is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can remove it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. International Data Transfers

Depending on your location and providers used, data may be processed in countries other than your own.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Effective date" above when changes are made.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us using the developer contact information listed on the app’s Google Play page.
